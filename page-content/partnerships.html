City Impact
Adopt A Building volunteers serve on Sundays from 2-5pm in the Tenderloin. A group from Epic visits residents in the same apartment building each week. They build relationships with people as they deliver food, share in conversation, and offer prayer. There are many other opportunities to use your talents and serve at City Impact. You can serve in the kitchen, thrift store, health and wellness center, food bank, social services center, TL made, and more. Email forthecity@epicsf.com if you are interested in serving or learning more.
Because Justice Matters
Manicure Outreach takes place every Monday afternoon from 1:00-4:30pm. Our volunteers provide free manicures, offer a listening ear and prayer to the women of the Tenderloin. We also set up an art table for the women to express their creativity, engage with each other and build new friendships and community. Read more and start the process to begin volunteering. For off-site, 1-time volunteer opportunities, we welcome you to join our street outreach, special events and/or lend us your professional skills (marketing, design, photography, videography, baking, etc.) Email Rhonda Poppen to start volunteering or to learn more about any of these opportunities.
Bessie Carmichael
Weekly opportunities to serve – Bessie Food Bank, Walking School Bus, and Elementary or Middle School tutoring One-time opportunities to serve include Family Fun Day and other special events. Walking School Bus Help Bessie kids get to school on time. Volunteers meet each Monday at 7:30 to walk (in a group) to get specific students from their home to school on time. Bessie Food Bank Each Thursday, SF Food Bank provides a food pantry on site at the school for families to receive groceries. We are looking for volunteers who can help set up and pass out the food each week from 7:30-9:00am. This is a great opportunity to serve before heading into the office! Tutoring and homework support at United Playaz United Playaz is a SOMA-based youth development organization based at 1038 Howard St. (Read more about them at http://unitedplayaz.org). Background checks done via United Playaz. Email Sharon Soucy to sign up to serve with Bessie Carmichael.

Church Planting Partnerships

Current SV
Mountain View, CA

Started in the fall of 2016, Current’s mission is love God, love one another, and love the world. Current wants to be invested in the common good of society, while also reflecting God’s love for the world.

Eden Church
Campbell, CA

Eden’s mission is to impact the Silicon Valley by leading people to become fully devoted followers of Christ. Eden focuses on impacting their community by serving schools and families.

Story City Church
Los Angeles, CA

Story City is a new church in Los Angeles with a mission to be a church for the story of God and the city of LA. Story City recently inherited a building that will serve as their base for ministry, offices, training, and students.

International Partnerships

Compassion International
Kampala, Uganda

Epic has partnered with Compassion International to help fund projects with United Christian Centre and Compassion Project. Individuals can sponsor kids in Kampala monthly to provide for basic needs and school fees.

Impact Church
Oradea, Romania

Impact Church exists to represent Christ and serve the community--their projects focus on serving the poor and disadvantaged, building community, and equipping the next generation of servant leaders in Romania.

LEC
Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

LEC's vision is to see a church formed in every one of Ho Chi Minh's 24 districts. They share the love and hope of Jesus through English clubs, soccer clinics, and reaching out to the poor.
