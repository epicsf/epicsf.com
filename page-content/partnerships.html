Local Partnerships

City Impact

City Impact intervenes to break the cycle of poverty and despair in the Tenderloin neighborhood. For over 30 years they have accomplished this by bringing relief for those in urgent need, rehabilitation for those wanting a way out, and development to ensure the cycles of poverty and despair are not repeated. They accomplish this through several ministries and outreaches. This fall City Impact Academy has 120 children enrolled in the school where they were able to launch two new special needs classrooms. The Health and Wellness Center was proud to start a partnership with Kaiser, helping 366 TL residents secure a primary health provider. City Impact continues outreach through the Rescue Mission, Adopt A Building, youth programs, and TL Made, a social enterprise.

Because Justice Matters

Because Justice Matters reaches women who have experienced sexual exploitation and domestic violence, and works to prevent the cycle of abuse faced by young girls. Since BJM started 7 years ago they have been building intentional relationships with women and seeking to support their healing process as well as introduce them to a relationship with Jesus. This summer BJM was invited into a local TL hotel to work with a very specific group of isolated women and children. It has been a powerful time of teaching ESL, offering parenting strategies and support, and being given the opportunity to build some deep and genuine relationships. BJM is excited to continue nail day outreach on Mondays, small healing groups and dance for young girls.

Bessie Carmichael

Bessie Carmichael is the only public school located in the SoMa neighborhood. The school educates almost 700 children, Kindergarten through 8th grade, on two campus. Epic began this partnership in October 2010 and has maintained it through resourcing teachers and students. Over the last five years, we have been able to provide basic supplies for teachers and students, ELMO projectors for each classroom, book bins for each student, and additional reading and teaching materials for students such as scholastic news and national geographic. In the last year, Epic has also provided tutors to help in the classroom on a weekly basis.

Church Planting Partnerships

City Church

City Church meets in the heart of Montreal, Quebec and is led by Pastor Chris McGregor. The church started weekly services in 2013 and just recently celebrated their 2nd birthday. City Church is committed to the life and teachings of Jesus and also committed to using those teachings to strengthen Montreal. In 2014, City Church started Love Montreal, an initiative created to fuel acts of kindness and service throughout the city. In May, a team from Epic traveled to Montreal to serve with City Church. The team had the opportunity to volunteer with others from the church at a major city event.

Email forthecity@epicsf.com for more information or to join the trip.

Front Range Christian Church

Front Range Christian Church is a new faith community in Castle Rock, Colorado. Front Range began meeting in February of 2014 and exists to help people become intentional neighbors through knowing and following Jesus. This spring they baptized 16 people during one of their baptism celebrations. Front Range is committed to serving those in need in their community and around the world. In addition to the two services at their main campus, Front Range launched a new campus on September 13 in an effort to reach more people in the community.

International Partnerships

United Christian Centre Kampala, Uganda

Epic partners with United Christian Centre, a local church in Kampala, through Compassion International. UCC is committed to transforming their community through providing education and care for children, equipping people with meaningful skills, and sharing the hope of Jesus. For the last 4 years, we have helped fund a beauty training school, a grinding mill, new classrooms for the primary school, and open heart surgery for a little girl. In addition to funding specific projects, over 110 individuals from Epic sponsor children in this region through Compassion.

Email forthecity@epicsf.com for more information or to join the trip.

Impact Church Oradea, Romania

Impact Church, led by Pastor Adi Galiger, started meeting 4.5 years ago in the center of Oradea, Romania. The church meets in a renovated factory and is making great strides to reach the younger generation through discipleship. Impact Church is also committed to serving the needs of the community and people around them. One of the ways they are doing this is by renovating hospitals in Oradea and providing them resources to care for patients. The church is also involved in serving state run orphanages to provide to a better quality of life for the children who live there. A team from Epic traveled to Oradea for the first time last May where they participated in painting and construction at one of the hospitals. The team was also able to visit orphanages and gypsy villages to experience how Impact Church is making a difference.

Email forthecity@epicsf.com for more information or to join the trip.

The Pearl House Accra, Ghana

The Pearl House is located just outside of Accra, Ghana and is dedicated to providing refuge for vulnerable girls, giving them freedom and hope for the present and future. The Pearl House offers a multi-year program of refuge and restoration that will include spiritual healing through Christ, classroom education, vocational training, and seed money for starting a new life. The Pearl House was able to purchase land this year and began building a permanent home for the girls to live in.
