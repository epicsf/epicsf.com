Join a Group Today!

We believe that life is meant to be shared and Epic Groups are an intentional way for people to do that. Joining a group is a great way for you to connect with others, grow in your faith, and serve the city. Groups are made up of 10-15 people and meet weekly to discuss the text and teaching from the Sunday service.

Leadership Opportunities

We believe that God intended for us to live out life in the context of community, that life is meant to be shared. By leading or hosting an Epic Group, you are playing a key role in creating an environment for people to connect to one another and grow spiritually.

Opportunities to get involved:

Epic Group Leader
Responsible for leading weekly meetings and overseeing the group.
Epic Group Co-leader
Assists in leading & connecting with group participants.
Epic Group Apprentice
Epic Group Leader in training.
Epic Group Host
Opens & prepares their home for an Epic Group to meet in.
If you are interested in leading or hosting a group, email Lindsey Lee at groups@epicsf.com.
