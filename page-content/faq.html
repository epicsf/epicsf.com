FAQ

Select any of the series in our message archive to either watch the video cast or listen to the podcast. You can also download and subscribe to the podcast.

When does Epic Church meet?

Epic meets every Sunday at 9:30 am and 11:00 am. See our Media page for the current series or our About page for additional information.

Where does Epic Church meet?

We meet at 250 Stevenson Street (off 3rd Street). When you arrive there will be Epic volunteers at the door to welcome you.

How do I get there? If I drive, where can I park?

If you are taking public transit you can get off at the Powell Street Station. You can't get to Epic from 4th Street; use 3rd Street instead.

For convenient parking, the Jessie Square Garage is located directly in front of our space. Parking is $10 for 1-2 hours. Epic will reimburse $10 toward your parking at Jessie Square. Please visit the Information Table in our lobby to present your parking ticket and receive reimbursement.

What can I expect from a service at Epic?

Our worship experience is designed to equip and inspire you to live life as Jesus designed it to be lived. You can expect a live band, upbeat music, and Biblical teaching that applies to everyday life. Our service usually lasts 60-75 minutes. Epic Church has great diversity. We believe you will feel welcomed and right at home, no matter your age, relationship status, or background.

What should I wear?

Most people will dress in casual weekend attire, so dress how you feel comfortable and you'll fit right in.

What about my children?

For children from birth to ten years old we provide an age-appropriate and engaging environment for your child that is fun and will teach them everyday values for life. For more information visit the Epic Kids page.

For more information, please contact us at info@epicsf.com.
